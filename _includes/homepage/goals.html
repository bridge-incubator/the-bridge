Our Goals

The Bridge funds initiatives through donations, grants, and fee-for-service engagements. Initiatives can come from anywhere! Whether from a government agency needing to modernize IT processes or from someone with a bold service delivery idea, The Bridge will work to get the support, funding, connections, and staff needed to deliver.

Once we have a fiscal sponsor in place we will post information about how to pitch an idea to The Bridge, donate money, volunteer, and indicate your availability to work as a contractor on projects.
