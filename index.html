What we do

The Bridge works with government agencies and nonprofits to improve the delivery of public services through a combination of human-centered design, agile development, collaboration, and coaching.

Who we are

We are a collective of former public servants with a track record of successfully tackling complex problems and improving government systems. Our consultants are available to work on initiatives funded through donations, grants, or fee-for-service engagements.

Work with us

The Bridge is seeking bold ideas for high-impact projects. If you have a venture that addresses a public need, we want to hear from you! We'll work with you to take your vision from concept to reality, providing the support, funding, and connections needed to launch it.

Support us

The Bridge is made possible via fiscal sponsorship from The Minneapolis Foundation. Donate to The Bridge to support our work.
